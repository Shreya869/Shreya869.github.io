My Entrepreneurship Journey

Entrepreneurship is a puzzle that I've recently discovered. In the process of figuring it out, I attended a 54-hour Techstars Startup Weekend which made me so freakin passionate about startups and led to SVAP being born. SVAP is a safety device which discreetly tested if any harmful substance is present in your drink when you go partying at the bars and clubs. Taking on this project taught me a lot about how startups work and the struggles that entrepreneurs face. Then I joined the E-Cell of the uni called TIEDC, which ever since has been my second home. Being here taught me what leadership and teamwork is actually about. It also helped me understand how to start something from scratch and see it being built to what it is today. This place made my inner leader come out and helped me strengthen my weaknesses and gave me a purpose. I am constantly learning because I never settle. So,now, this passion remains with me. Ther's certainly yet more to learn, yet more problems to solve, and yet more to build. And for this, I am very grateful.
